Cube Run

In this 3D cube puzzle game, the player must solve increasingly complex levels by strategically using three different cubes. Each cube has unique properties or abilities that can help the player overcome obstacles, such as changing size, shifting colors, or interacting with the environment in different ways. The player must think logically and plan their moves carefully, as each level presents new challenges that require the cubes to work together in creative ways to unlock paths, activate switches, or break barriers. The game encourages critical thinking and problem-solving, with each level requiring a new approach to progress. The 3D environment adds depth to the puzzles, providing a rich, immersive experience for players who enjoy logical challenges.

Online Shooter

In this cinematic AAA-quality game scene, soldiers find themselves fighting for survival against a relentless horde of zombies in a ruined city at dusk. The battle centers around a battle-worn BTR-82A, reinforced with sandbags and makeshift armor, as it becomes the last line of defense. Soldiers take cover behind debris, unleashing gunfire to hold back the undead. The horde is overwhelming, with some zombies sprinting toward the soldiers, while others are grotesquely mutated, including a massive juggernaut clad in riot gear. The chaos is heightened by explosions, fires, and tracer rounds lighting up the smoke-filled battlefield. Distant helicopters and drones fly overhead, scanning the devastation. The atmosphere is one of pure tension, as the soldiers face an impossible fight in a desperate last-stand survival scenario.

Whispers In The Void

Whispers in the Void is a psychological horror game set in a dark, atmospheric universe. The player takes on the role of a lone protagonist, trapped within a mysterious and seemingly endless void. As they navigate through this eerie space, they uncover fragmented memories and cryptic messages, all while being pursued by sinister forces that lurk in the darkness. The game focuses on exploration, puzzle-solving, and survival, with a heavy emphasis on narrative and atmosphere. The deeper the player ventures into the void, the more unsettling the environment becomes, as reality bends and distorts, leading to a descent into madness. With minimal combat, the game relies on tension, auditory cues, and visual storytelling to create a chilling experience. Each whisper in the void draws the player closer to uncovering the truth, but at what cost to their sanity?

Tower Defence

In this 2D tower defense game, enemies aim to destroy your castle, and your goal is to stop them by strategically placing turrets along their path. The turrets have a simple but crucial difference: some fire faster, while others fire slower. Each turret type must be positioned wisely to maximize damage, as faster-firing turrets can quickly take down weaker enemies, while slower-firing turrets may be better for taking out tougher foes. As waves of enemies increase in strength and numbers, you'll need to adjust your strategy and turret placements to ensure your castle remains safe. It's a fast-paced game of timing and strategy to prevent the enemy from reaching and destroying your castle.
